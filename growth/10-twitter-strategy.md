# X (Twitter) Growth Strategy

> A comprehensive 90-day content strategy for PilotStack on X (Twitter). Designed to build brand authority, drive engagement, and generate qualified traffic.

---

## 1. Strategy Overview

### Why X/Twitter for PilotStack?

- **Direct access to SaaS community** — Founders, marketers, and developers actively discuss tools on X
- **Rapid feedback loops** — Test content ideas and messaging before investing in long-form content
- **Relationship building** — Engage with journalists, investors, and potential partners
- **Search visibility** — Tweets rank in Google for brand and topic searches

### Account Setup Checklist

- [ ] Professional profile photo (PilotStack logo or founder headshot)
- [ ] Banner image with value proposition (1200x600px)
- [ ] Bio: Clear description + link + relevant hashtags
- [ ] Pinned tweet: Best-performing content or launch thread
- [ ] Link in bio: PilotStack landing page with UTM tracking
- [ ] Profile completed (location, website, join date visible)
- [ ] List created: "SaaS Community" — key accounts to monitor
- [ ] List created: "Competitors" — track industry landscape
- [ ] List created: "Press & Media" — journalists covering software

### Content Pillars

| Pillar | % of Content | Description |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| Data & Statistics | 25% | Original insights from PilotStack review data |
| Comparisons | 20% | Tool vs tool, side-by-side analysis |
| Industry Insights | 20% | Trends, predictions, observations |
| Product Tips | 15% | How to use reviews, evaluation frameworks |
| Behind the Scenes | 10% | Building in public, founder journey |
| Community Engagement | 10% | Retweets, replies, quote tweets |

## 3. Launch Thread Structure

### Sample Launch Thread

**Tweet 1:** We built PilotStack so you never have to guess which software to buy again. Our mission: make software buying decisions transparent, data-driven, and community-powered. Let me show you how it works. 🧵

**Tweet 2:** The problem: Most software buyers choose based on homepage copy, not real user experience. Review sites are fragmented, inconsistent, and often biased. We wanted to fix that. [screenshot of problem]

**Tweet 3:** PilotStack aggregates reviews from real users — verified, categorized, and searchable. You can filter by company size, industry, use case, and more. No more wondering if a review applies to YOUR situation.

**Tweet 4:** Here's what makes us different: 1) Verified reviews only 2) Video reviews supported 3) Side-by-side comparison tools 4) Transparent scoring methodology [visual comparison]

**Tweet 5:** We analyzed 10,000+ reviews to build our recommendation engine. Every time you search, you get personalized results based on your specific needs. No one-size-fits-all rankings.

**Tweet 6:** For software companies: PilotStack gives you a direct line to user feedback. Respond to reviews, gather insights, and showcase your product to buyers who are actively researching.

**Tweet 7:** We're launching in beta today with 500+ products across 50 categories. Including: Project Management, CRM, Marketing, Analytics, Design, Developer Tools, and more.

**Tweet 8:** Try PilotStack for free at [URL] — no credit card required. We're just getting started and would love your feedback. What categories should we add next?

**Tweet 9:** Special thanks to our early users who helped shape the product. Your feedback has been invaluable. We're just getting started. 🚀
