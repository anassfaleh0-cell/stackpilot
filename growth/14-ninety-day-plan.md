# 90-Day Growth Execution Plan

> A week-by-week tactical execution plan for PilotStack's growth marketing initiatives. This plan integrates directory submissions, content distribution, outreach, and amplification across all channels.

---

## Execution Overview

| Phase | Weeks | Focus Area | Primary Channels | Effort Level |
|-------|-------|-----------|------------------|--------------|
| **Foundation** | 1-2 | Setup, profiles, first submissions | Directories, Reddit | High setup |
| **Content Distribution** | 3-4 | Syndication, social launch | Medium, X/Twitter, LinkedIn | Medium |
| **Outreach** | 5-6 | Link building, guest posting | Email, directories | High |
| **Scale** | 7-8 | Volume push, podcast, Reddit | All | Very High |
| **Amplify** | 9-10 | PR, interviews, long-form | LinkedIn, PR, podcast | High |
| **Measure & Optimize** | 11-12 | Audit, optimize, plan | All (analysis) | Medium |

| Activity | Weekly Hours | Who |

> The key to this plan is **consistency over intensity**. It's better to post 5 thoughtful comments on Reddit every day than 35 comments once a week. It's better to send 10 well-researched outreach emails than 50 templated ones. Build the systems, track the metrics, and iterate based on data. Good luck.
