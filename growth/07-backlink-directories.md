## PilotStack Qualification Assessment

PilotStack qualifies for listings under:

1. **Software Review Platforms** — Core category across G2, Capterra, GetApp, TrustRadius
2. **SaaS Directories** — Given it's a SaaS product reviewing other SaaS products
3. **AI Tool Directories** — If any AI-powered features exist (recommendation engine, sentiment analysis)
4. **Developer Tools** — If offering API access or technical integrations
5. **Startup Directories** — As a growing startup itself

**Note on Qualification:** Most software review directories require the listed product to have a free tier, trial, or demo. Ensure PilotStack offers at least one of these before submitting. For directories requiring "verified reviews," engage existing users to submit reviews in exchange for featuring their reviews prominently.
